ABOUT TALKS & WORKSHOPS

WHAT?

Learning is always interesting. As part of our mission, we have created Talks & Workshops. Here you will find a space where experts will be invited to share their knowledge about the most edge-cutting topics in technology.

WHERE?

Due to our goal to include as many people as possible, T&W will take place online. The talks will be streamed live on Twitch and discussion will take place afterwards in our Discord server. Each T&W will be recorded on YouTube.

WHEN?

During the semester, we will organize these events periodically. With each T&W it's date and time will be announced. Generally, they will take place at 7 p.m. (GMT+1). As the dates vary we recommend to anyone interested to subscribe to our email list in order to get information of our future talks or workshops.
