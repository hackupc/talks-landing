ABOUT TALKS & WORKSHOPS

WHAT?

Learning is always interesting. As part of our mission, we have created Talks & Workshops. Here you will find a space where an expert will be invited to explain any topic, mostly related to computer science.

WHERE?

Due to our goal to bring it as many people as possible, T&W will take place Online. Using Twitch to stream the speakers' explanation and Discord to discuss after they ends (or wherever you want).

WHEN?

With each T&W it's date will be announced. Generally, they will take place at 7 p.m. (GMT+2). If you can't participate, don't worry! Each T&W will be recorded on YouTube. And you can use ours Discord to ask to the community.
